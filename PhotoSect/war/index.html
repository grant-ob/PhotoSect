Welcome to the PhotoSect:
Photo Sharing Application!


PhotoSect is an application in which users can view and comment on images that have been shared by themselves or other PhotoSect users!

Account users can add, delete, and tag their own images, and can comment on any image. Also, account users can easily find other images by sorting on tags, username, view count, or comment count.

Non-account users can browse images and can perform sorting to find images, but cannot add, delete, comment on, or tag images.

PhotoSect was created by Nicholas Noel, Grant O'Brien, and Adam Sheppard.
